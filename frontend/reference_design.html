Curator's Advice

Mood Consistency

Maintaining a consistent mood is key. Consider tracks with similar emotional weight.

  • Analyze lyrical themes and tonality.
  • Match instrumentation styles and textures.
  • Keep BPMs within a close range (e.g., ±10 BPM).

Energy Flow

Guide the listener's energy. Build up intensity gradually or create waves of calm and excitement.

  • Start with lower energy or atmospheric tracks.
  • Introduce a peak track or moment strategically.
  • Conclude with a resolving or calming song.

Discovery & Surprise

An occasional unexpected gem can delight listeners and broaden their musical horizons.

  • Include a lesser-known artist or indie track.
  • Introduce a slightly different but related sub-genre.
  • Vary track length or structure occasionally.
